We pre-announce the Third International Heinz von Foerster Congress. The congress will take place from 16–18 November 2007 at the University of Vienna. The overall motto will be “systems/systemics.” In addition, there will be two special sections, one on Ernst von Glasersfeld and his work on the occasion of Ernst’s 90th birthday, and one on the life and work of Gordon Pask, on the occasion of the opening of the Gordon Pask Archive. More detailed information will be available soon.

On behalf of the Heinz von Foerster Society,
