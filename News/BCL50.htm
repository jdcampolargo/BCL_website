BCL @ 50

The American Society for Cybernetics and the Center for Complex Systems Research, University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign (UIUC), will commemorate the 50th anniversary of the founding of the Biological Computer Laboratory at the Eighth Understanding Complex Systems Symposium, 12–15 May 2008, at UIUC.

In addition to this web site, follow the ASC events listing or the UCS2008 site for updates.
